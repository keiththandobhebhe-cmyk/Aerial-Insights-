What We Offer

Three Core Services

Each service is built on the ADI Framework — Signal, Pattern, Insight, Decision — to ensure you stay in charge of AI, not the other way around.

🎓

Service 01

ADI Workshops

2–3 hour group sessions teaching the ADI Framework — a clear, structured practice for using AI as a thinking partner, not a crutch.

Who It's For

Students, teams, entrepreneurship cells, university departments

What They Learn

  • Signal: Notice what's off. What's missing.
  • Pattern: Let AI find connections you can't see.
  • Insight: Own the meaning.
  • Decision: Own the choice.
Outcome

Participants leave with a clear practice for using AI as a thinking partner, not a crutch.

Contact for pricing · Group rates available

Service 02

AI Decision Audits

1–2 week assessment of how an organization is using AI for decisions — uncovering blind trust, hallucinations, bias, and errors.

Who It's For

Companies, startups, university departments

What We Do

  • Map current AI use across the organization
  • Identify where AI is trusted blindly
  • Find hallucinations, bias, and errors
  • Recommend verification framework
Outcome

A clear report of risks and a practical plan to address them.
